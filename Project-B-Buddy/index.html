BUDDY CREATOR

You can generate a cool buddy with this website!!!

When you proceed you will see a canvas which you can use to create a character with customizable body parts!

Instruction: You can choose the shape of the body, arms, legs and head you want based on the shapes provided. Once you have selected a shape, you may click the "Next" button to choose another body part. You can always use the "Back" button to move back and reselect any body part. Each arm and leg moves differently ! So different combinations of arms and different legs will give you different ways to move! Use your imagination to create a super cool buddy! Oh, by the way, when you have selected all the body parts, you can save your buddy through the "Save" button.
